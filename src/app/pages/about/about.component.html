Crowdfunding campaigns

Anyone with a Tezos wallet can create or support a crowdfunding campaign easily. No account, just a wallet. When a campaign is created, a new smart contract is deployed. This smart contract is yours and the platform has no control over it, which means only you can make decisions about your campaign - a truly decentralized crowdfunding campaign without intermediaries.

Funds are immediately transferred from the supporter wallet to the campaign owner wallet and there is no commission per transaction. As a campaign owner, you only need to transfer 3 upon campaign creation - this is to prevent spam campaigns and support the platform's costs.

 Fund a campaign

To fund a campaign, simply go to the page of the campaign you want to support and define how much you want to donate. The owner of the campaign will receive your funds instantly, when the transaction is complete.

Collect funds

There's no need to collect funds because all funds are instantly transferred to the campaign owner.

 Closing a campaign

Your campaign will be active and receiving funds unless you close it. You can close your campaign anytime, even if you don't reach your goal.

FAQ

I lost access to my wallet, what can I do?

Every time a user funds your campaign, the funds will be immediately transferred to the wallet that created the campaign. If you lose access to your wallet, you won't be able to access those funds and there is no way of closing the campaign to prevent users from funding it further. Please make sure you have your wallet secured.
