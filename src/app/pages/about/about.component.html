Crowdfunding campaigns

Anyone with a Tezos wallet can create or support a crowdfunding campaign easily. No account, just a wallet. When a campaign is created, a new smart contract is deployed. This smart contract is yours and the platform has no control over it, which means only you can make decisions about your campaign - a truly decentralized crowdfunding campaign without intermediaries.

Funds are immediately transferred from the supporter wallet to the campaign owner wallet and there is no commission per transaction. As a campaign owner, you only need to transfer 3 upon campaign creation - this is to prevent spam campaigns and to support the platform costs.

Create a crowdfunding campaign

You can create a crowdfunding campaign here. This will deploy a new contract for your campaign, where all the needed info will be stored. You'll need to connect your wallet, which will be associated with your campaign - make sure you have access to this wallet.

You'll need to define:

{{element.name}} {{element.description}}

To prevent spam campaigns and to support costs with the platform, you'll be asked to transfer 3.

Now that your campaign is live, share it with your friends, family and in social media!

 Fund a campaign

To fund a campaign, simply go to the page of the campaign you want to support and define how much you want to donate. The owner of the campaign will receive your funds immediately, when the transaction is complete.

Collect funds

There's no need to collect funds because all funds are immediately transferred to the campaign owner.

 Closing a campaign

Your campaign will be active and receiving funds unless you close it. You can close your campaign anytime, even if you don't reach your goal.

FAQ

I lost access to my wallet, what can I do?

Everytime a user funds your campaign, the funds will be immediately transfered to the wallet that created the campaign. If you lost access to your wallet, you won't be able to access those funds and there is no way of closing the campaign to prevent users from funding it further. Please make sure you have your wallet secured.
